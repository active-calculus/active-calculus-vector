Let \(\vF(x,y) = \langle y^2,2xy+3\rangle\text{.}\)

  1. Let \(C_1\) be the portion of the graph of \(y=x^3-x\) from \((1,0)\) to \((-1,0)\text{.}\) Calculate \(\int_{C_1}\vF\cdot d\vr\text{.}\)

  2. Let \(C_2\) be the line segment from \((1,0)\) to \((-1,0)\text{.}\) Calculate \(\int_{C_2}\vF\cdot d\vr\text{.}\)

  3. Let \(C_3\) be the circle of radius \(3\) centered at the origin, oriented counterclockwise. Calculate \(\oint_{C_3} \vF\cdot d\vr\text{.}\)
